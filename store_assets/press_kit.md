## Product Overview

### What is Allma?
Allma is a revolutionary AI companion platform that creates personalized, emotionally intelligent relationships between users and their custom AI companions. Unlike traditional chatbots, Allma companions possess unique personalities, remember conversations, and adapt to each user's communication style while maintaining the highest standards of safety and privacy.

### Key Innovation Points
- **Personality-Driven AI**: Each companion has a unique personality based on the Big Five psychological model
- **Advanced Memory System**: Hierarchical memory formation that creates lasting, meaningful relationships
- **Privacy-First Architecture**: All data encrypted locally with zero-knowledge cloud storage
- **Comprehensive Safety**: Multi-layered content moderation and mental health crisis detection
- **Emotional Intelligence**: Real-time mood detection and empathetic response generation

## FAQs

### Is Allma safe for users?
Yes, Allma includes comprehensive safety features including real-time content moderation, mental health crisis detection, and ethical AI guidelines enforcement.

### How is user privacy protected?
All conversations are encrypted locally on the user's device. Allma follows a privacy-first architecture with no data sharing and complete user control over their information.

### What makes Allma different from other AI chatbots?
Allma companions have deep, consistent personalities based on psychological research, advanced memory systems, and focus on safety and privacy.

### Is Allma appropriate for children?
Allma is rated 12+ and includes age-appropriate content filtering. We recommend parental guidance for users under 18.

### Will Allma replace human relationships?
No, Allma is designed to supplement and enhance human relationships, not replace them. We actively promote healthy boundaries and real-world connections.
